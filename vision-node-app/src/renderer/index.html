How Rewards Work

Your node earns VCN rewards by maintaining uptime and providing storage to the network.

Rewards are calculated based on your node weight (determined by class and mode) multiplied by uptime duration.

Desktop nodes running in full WiFi mode receive 0.02x weight, the highest among non-agent nodes.
